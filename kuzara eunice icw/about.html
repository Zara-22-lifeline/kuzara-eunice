Our Services

Curriculum Development

We design comprehensive, standards-aligned curricula that balance academic rigor with real-world relevance. Our programs integrate STEM, arts, humanities, and life skills.

Student Support Services

We offer tutoring, mentoring, counseling, and special education services to ensure every student receives the individualized support they need to succeed.

Background Information

Founded in 2001, AGA KHAN INTERTERNATINAL emerged from a vision to transform traditional education systems into dynamic, student-focused learning ecosystems.

Our journey began when a group of passionate educators, researchers, and community leaders recognized the urgent need to bridge the gap between conventional teaching methods and the evolving needs of 21st-century learners.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student has access to transformative education that unlocks their full potential, fosters lifelong learning, and prepares them to become innovative, compassionate global citizens who can address the challenges of tomorrow.

We envision schools as vibrant communities where curiosity thrives, diversity is celebrated, and every individual is empowered to make meaningful contributions to society.

Objectives of the School

  • Integrate Technology:Leverage digital tools and platforms to create engaging, interactive learning experiences
  • Foster Critical Thinking:Develop curriculum that emphasizes problem-solving, analysis, and creative thinking skills
  • Build Inclusive Communities:Create welcoming environments that respect and value diversity in all its forms
  • Measure Impact: Use comprehensive assessment methods to track student progress and continuously improve our programs
  • Engage Families:Partner with parents and guardians as active participants in the educational journey
  • Promote Well-being:Support the social-emotional development and mental health of all students
